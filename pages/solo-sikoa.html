Biography

Solo Sikoa has emerged as one of the most intimidating forces in WWE. Known as "The Enforcer" of The Bloodline, Solo brings a unique intensity and ruthlessness that sets him apart from his family members. Despite being the younger brother of Jimmy and Jey Uso, Solo has quickly established his own identity in WWE.

As of September 2025, Solo has taken on a leadership role within The Bloodline faction, serving as Roman Reigns' most trusted lieutenant. His stoic demeanor and brutal in-ring style have made him a feared competitor across the SmackDown roster.

Solo's wrestling style is heavily influenced by his Samoan heritage, combining power moves with surprising agility. His adoption of the Samoan Spike as a finishing move—a tribute to his uncle Umaga—has proven to be devastatingly effective. Unlike his more flamboyant brothers, Solo is a man of few words who lets his actions speak for him.

Since his main roster debut in 2022, Solo has been steadily climbing the ranks in WWE. His unwavering loyalty to Roman Reigns and The Bloodline has positioned him as a key player in one of wrestling's most dominant factions. As he continues to evolve as a performer, many predict Solo will eventually break out as a major singles star in his own right.

Championship History

  • WWE United States Championship (1 time)
  • NXT North American Championship (1 time)

While Solo's championship resume is still growing, his impact in WWE extends far beyond title reigns. As The Bloodline's enforcer, he has been instrumental in helping the faction maintain its dominance across WWE.
