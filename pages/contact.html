Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us and booking WWE Superstars

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. For urgent matters related to upcoming events, please indicate this in your subject line, and we'll do our best to prioritize your request.

What information should I include in my booking request?

To help us process your booking request efficiently, please include the following information: event date, location, type of event, expected number of attendees, preferred WWE Superstar(s), and your budget range. The more details you provide, the better we can assist you.

Do you offer virtual appearances or video messages?

Yes, we offer virtual appearances and personalized video messages from WWE Superstars. These options are perfect for events with geographic constraints or smaller budgets. Please specify in your inquiry that you're interested in a virtual appearance, and we'll provide the available options.

Can I request specific WWE Superstars for my event?

Yes, you can request specific WWE Superstars for your event. However, availability depends on their schedule, including WWE live events, television tapings, and other commitments. We recommend providing multiple options in your request to increase the chances of securing a Superstar for your event.

What are your payment and cancellation policies?

Our standard policy requires a 50% deposit to secure the booking, with the remaining balance due 14 days before the event. Cancellations made more than 30 days before the event receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations between 14-30 days receive a 50% refund of the deposit. Cancellations less than 14 days before the event are non-refundable. Detailed terms will be provided in your booking contract.
