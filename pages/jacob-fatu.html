Biography

Jacob Fatu is one of the most intimidating and athletically gifted superstars in WWE history. Nicknamed "The Samoan Werewolf," Fatu combines raw power with shocking agility that defies his 300-pound frame. His ability to perform moonsaults and other high-flying maneuvers despite his size has made him one of the most unique performers in professional wrestling.

As of September 2025, Jacob is a key member of The Bloodline faction on SmackDown, having joined WWE in late 2024 after a dominant run in Major League Wrestling (MLW) where he was their World Heavyweight Champion for over 800 days. His arrival in WWE sent shockwaves through the roster, with his brutal style and imposing presence immediately establishing him as a top-tier threat.

Jacob's wrestling style is a terrifying blend of power moves, surprising agility, and Samoan fighting techniques. His wild, untamed persona and unpredictable nature make him one of the most feared competitors in WWE. As part of The Bloodline, he serves as a devastating weapon in Roman Reigns' arsenal, often unleashed to cause maximum destruction to their enemies.

Despite being relatively new to WWE, Jacob Fatu's impact has been immediate and substantial. His family lineage as part of the legendary Anoa'i wrestling dynasty gives him an instant connection to WWE's rich history, while his unique in-ring style points to an exciting future as one of the company's most dominant forces.

Championship History

  • MLW World Heavyweight Championship (1 time, 819 days)
  • MLW National Openweight Championship (1 time)
  • PCW ULTRA Heavyweight Championship (1 time)
  • WWE Intercontinental Championship (1 time)

Jacob Fatu's championship resume includes one of the longest world title reigns in modern wrestling history during his time in MLW. Since joining WWE, he has already captured the Intercontinental Championship, defeating Jey Uso in a brutal match that showcased his devastating power and surprising agility.
