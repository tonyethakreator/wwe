WWE United States Championship

Sami Zayn

24+ days • 3rd reign

Won on SmackDown (August 29, 2025)

The WWE United States Championship has a rich history dating back to its origins in the NWA/WCW. It was integrated into WWE in 2001 following the purchase of WCW and has since become one of WWE's most prestigious midcard championships.
