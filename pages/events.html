Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a WWE Superstar for my event?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance to ensure availability. WWE Superstars have busy schedules with live events, television tapings, and other commitments. For major events or popular Superstars, even more advance notice may be necessary.

Can I choose which WWE Superstar appears at my event?

Yes, you can request specific WWE Superstars for your event. We recommend providing multiple options as availability depends on the Superstar's schedule. Our team will work with you to secure your preferred choices whenever possible.

What is included in the pricing?

Our pricing includes the Superstar's appearance fee and basic coordination. Additional costs may include travel expenses, accommodation, security requirements, and any special requests. We provide a detailed quote after reviewing your specific event needs.

Do WWE Superstars perform wrestling matches at private events?

WWE Superstars do not perform wrestling matches at private events due to safety, insurance, and contractual reasons. Their appearances focus on meet and greets, photo opportunities, autograph sessions, speeches, and interactive activities appropriate for the event type.

What happens after I submit my booking request?

After submitting your request, our team will contact you within 2-3 business days to discuss your event in more detail. We'll check Superstar availability, provide pricing information, and guide you through the booking process. Once confirmed, you'll receive a formal contract outlining all details.
